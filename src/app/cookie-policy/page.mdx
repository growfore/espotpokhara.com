## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you interact with the site, and improve your overall browsing experience.

<Callout type="info">
  Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. They are uniquely assigned to your browser and can only be read by the website that set them.
</Callout>

## Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. This includes:

- Remembering your preferences and settings
- Analysing site traffic to improve our content and services
- Ensuring the website functions correctly
- Understanding which pages are most useful to our visitors

This helps us serve you better and continuously improve our website.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as navigation and access to secure areas of the site. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly.

### Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about pages visited, time spent on the site, and other browsing patterns. This data helps us improve our website and services.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics — distinguishes users | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics — distinguishes users | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics — throttles request rate | 1 minute |

### Preference Cookies

These remember your choices such as language preferences and display settings, providing a more personalised experience.

## Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

<Callout type="warning">
  If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not work as intended. You can usually find cookie settings in your browser's "Preferences" or "Settings" menu.
</Callout>

**How to manage cookies in popular browsers:**

1. **Google Chrome** — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
2. **Mozilla Firefox** — Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
3. **Safari** — Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
4. **Microsoft Edge** — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies
