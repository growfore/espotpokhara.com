## What We Offer

We provide end-to-end support for your study abroad journey, from the first consultation to post-arrival assistance. Our team has helped hundreds of students from Nepal achieve their dream of studying at top universities worldwide.

<Callout type="info">
  All our services are available in-person at our Pokhara office and through online consultations for students outside Pokhara.
</Callout>

### University Counseling

We help you identify the right university and program based on your academic background, career goals, and budget preferences. Our counselors provide personalized recommendations across multiple destinations including Japan, Canada, Australia, and the USA.

Key aspects of our counseling service:

- Academic profile assessment
- University shortlisting based on your preferences
- Course and program recommendations
- Scholarship and financial aid guidance
- Career outcome analysis

### Visa Guidance

Our experienced team provides step-by-step assistance with your visa application to maximize approval chances. We handle documentation, interview preparation, and application review.

### Language Classes

We offer comprehensive language training including JLPT preparation for Japan and English proficiency courses. Our classes are designed for all proficiency levels from beginner to advanced.

**Available programs:**

- **JLPT N5–N2** — Comprehensive Japanese language training
- **IELTS Preparation** — Academic and General Training
- **PTE Academic** — Computer-based test preparation
- **English for Daily Life** — Conversation and communication skills

### Scholarship Assistance

We help you identify and apply for scholarships, grants, and financial aid opportunities to make your study abroad dream affordable.

> "I never thought I could afford to study abroad until Espot helped me find and apply for the MEXT scholarship. Now I'm pursuing my Master's in Japan."
> — Former student

### Test Preparation

Structured preparation for standardized tests with experienced instructors, mock tests, and personalized study plans. Our test preparation programs include:

- Diagnostic assessment to identify strengths and weaknesses
- Customized study plans based on your target score
- Weekly mock tests with detailed performance analysis
- One-on-one doubt-clearing sessions
- Tips and strategies for each test section

### Pre-Departure Orientation

Comprehensive orientation sessions covering cultural adaptation, accommodation arrangements, travel planning, and academic preparation for your destination country.

Topics covered:

1. Cultural norms and etiquette of your destination country
2. Accommodation options and booking assistance
3. Travel planning and flight booking tips
4. Packing checklist for international students
5. Banking, insurance, and SIM card setup
6. Emergency contacts and support networks
